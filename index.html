Welcome to ClubFinder!

This website is meant to help you find which club on the MSJ campus is right for you. You will be asked a few questions about what interests you have, and we will then recommend you to clubs at MSJ. You will be shown a profile for each club, that will include a rating out of 5 stars and reviews from students and staff based on their experiences with the club. Sign up now to keep up with the clubs at your school!
